ABOUT OPENPRICE

Openprice is a mobile app that digitizes receipts, transforming them into clean, organized, and easy-to-navigate e-receipts. You can browse, search, and organize your receipts. Keeping track of your personal purchase history has never been easier!

Your shopping lists are auto-generated for convenience, and are based on your shopping trends, relevant deals, and similar users. You can add and remove items, or even create a brand new list.

Openprice has an extensive database of real-time items prices from over 100? stores. We use this information to help you save money by notifying you of pricedrops and hot new deals.

Openprice also lets you share the deals you have found with your friends, so they can enjoy that crazy sale on cupcakes at your local store too!
