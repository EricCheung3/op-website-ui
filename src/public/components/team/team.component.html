MEET THE TEAM

Hengshuai Yao

CEO & Founder

Hengshuai believes Machine Learning can make people's lives better. That is why he decided to pursue his idea behind openprice. Hengshuai's responsibilities include managing the team and working on the back-end to ensure clean results.

Hengshuai is a PhD candidate at University of Alberta. He has 10+ years of research experience in Reinforcement Learning and Machine Learning, and has won numerous awards for his work including placing 4th at the World RoboCup Simulation League in 2004.

While working at Yahoo! Hengshuai won the Yahoo! CEO award for his work on "Early Trending", bringing detection time of trending topics down from 97 hours to 1.5 hours.

Yuan Ji

CTO & Co-Founder

Yuan is a passionate learner and computer programmer who believes clean code and craftsmanship is the foundation of a great software product.

He is the architect of openprice. He works on all major parts of the product including cloud infrastructure, back-end Restful API, mobile and web front end.

Yuan got his master degree in Computing Science at the University of Alberta, and has been as professional enterprise application developer for 15 years.

In his spare time, he is an open source evangelist. He has participated in a number of open source projects, and won the Spring Champion in 2013 by contributing to the Spring Social Security Project.
